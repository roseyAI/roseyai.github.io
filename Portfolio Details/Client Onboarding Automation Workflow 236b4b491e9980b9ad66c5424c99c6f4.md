### Client Onboarding Automation Workflow

**Problem:** Busy teams waste hours on repetitive onboarding tasks - creating folders, updating spreadsheets, sending welcome emails, and manual notifications.

**Solution:** This automated workflow eliminates manual work by connecting your existing Google Suite tools. When a new client submits a form, the system automatically:

- Creates organized client folders
- Adds client data to your tracking database
- Sends personalized welcome emails
- Updates team spreadsheets
- Triggers internal notifications
- Sends progress updates via Telegram

**Built for:** Teams using Google Suite who want to save time on small but time-consuming manual tasks.

**Benefits:**

- Fully customizable to your business needs
- Scalable as your client base grows
- Works with tools you already use
- Saves 2-3 hours per client onboarding

**Tech Stack:** Make.com, Google Forms, Google Sheets, Gmail, Telegram
