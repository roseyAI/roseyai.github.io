## The Problem

Customer service teams are overwhelmed with inquiries, leading to delayed responses and missed opportunities to address negative feedback before it escalates.

## The Solution

An automated Telegram chatbot that intelligently triages customer messages using sentiment analysis. Positive messages receive instant AI-powered responses, while negative messages trigger immediate email alerts to human support staff - ensuring no unhappy customer goes unnoticed.

- **Start Sentiment Detection**: Automatically analyzes incoming messages to determine customer mood
- **Instant Positive Response**: Provides immediate, helpful replies to satisfied customers
- **Negative Feedback Alerts**: Sends real-time email notifications to team members when issues arise
- **Seamless Integration**: Built on Make.com for reliable automation and easy maintenance
- Different languages supported (using Gemini LLM)
- Can be added to groups

## Future Customization Options (scalable!)

### 1. **Custom AI Prompts**

Businesses can tailor the chatbot's personality and response style to match their brand voice - from formal banking responses to casual e-commerce interactions.

### 2. **FAQ Integration**

Add a knowledge base node that pulls answers from company-specific FAQ documents, ensuring accurate and consistent information delivery.

### 3. **Multi-Channel Routing**

Expand beyond sentiment analysis with intelligent routing based on keywords, urgency levels, or customer tiers - directing VIP customers to priority queues or technical issues to specialized teams.
