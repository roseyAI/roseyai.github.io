About the Program

The AI Advantage for Professionals BOOST CAMP was created to:

  • Learn about advanced prompt engineering and the essentials of AI agents in an accessible and interactive format, with instructor Aishwarya Ramakrishnan, a multi-awarded AI professional.
  • Build your own AI Agent: get step-by-step guidance on how to build your own agents with low-code tools.

Earning Criteria

The Certified Signature Programs represent a suite of educational offerings tailored by the dedicated volunteers of She Loves Data, who are not only professionals but also seasoned practitioners in their respective fields.

All the certificates issued for this program require a demonstration of proficiency and willingness to learn: Certificate of Achievement with Distinction is issued to participants who had scored at least 70% on both assessments of the course, and averaged 85% or more for the combined scores.

These rigorous standards maintain the certification's prestige, ensuring it stands as a respected symbol of the participant's acquired expertise.
