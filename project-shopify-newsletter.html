Shopify Liquid Customizations

Shopify customizations to improve lead generation

The Problem:

Prestige Theme default newsletter form only collects an email address, which limits personalization and segmentation. There's no built-in option for capturing first and last names — something most email marketing tools benefit from.

My Solution:

We added two supported fields — contact[first_name] and contact[last_name] — directly to the newsletter form. These are recognized by Shopify and passed properly to their CRM.

Result:

Visitors can now enter their first name, last name, and email in your newsletter form. Shopify stores this information under the customer's profile. Your email marketing tools (e.g., ActiveCampaign) receive full contact details without extra setup.

Why This Matters:

This small update improves personalization, supports better email segmentation, and makes your list far more useful — without requiring extra scripts or apps.

Tools Used:

  • Shopify (Prestige Theme)
  • Liquid templating
  • Native Shopify field support

Benefits:

  • Better Personalization: Collect names along with email addresses for more personalized email campaigns
  • Enhanced Segmentation: Use first and last names to segment your audience more effectively
  • No Extra Apps Required: Uses native Shopify fields, no third-party apps needed
  • Seamless CRM Integration: Data flows directly to your email marketing tools like ActiveCampaign
  • Improved Lead Quality: More complete contact information means better quality leads
