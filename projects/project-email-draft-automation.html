AI Email Draft Generator

n8n Microsoft Outlook OpenAI

Problem

  • High volume of emails requiring personalized responses
  • Time-consuming to draft professional replies for each message
  • Difficult to prioritize and organize incoming emails
  • Repetitive email writing takes focus away from core tasks

Solution

Created an intelligent email assistant that runs hourly, checks three designated Outlook folders (Review, To Respond, Greg), analyzes unread emails, and generates professional draft responses using GPT-4o. Drafts are automatically created in Outlook and original emails moved to an organized folder for final review.

Benefits

  • Saves 2-3 hours daily on email drafting
  • Professional, consistent tone across all responses
  • Automatic email organization and prioritization
  • Drafts ready for quick review and send
  • Never miss an email that needs response

How It Works

  1. 1
    Schedule trigger runs every hour

    Automated execution ensures timely responses to incoming emails.

  2. 2
    Retrieves emails from three monitored Outlook folders

    Checks "Review," "To Respond," and "1. Greg" folders for messages needing replies.

  3. 3
    Merges all emails into single queue

    Combines emails from multiple folders for unified processing.

  4. 4
    OpenAI Chat Model (GPT-4o) analyzes each email

    AI reads subject, body preview, and sender information to understand context.

  5. 5
    AI Agent generates professional draft response

    Creates business-appropriate reply with proper greeting, acknowledgment, and helpful content.

  6. 6
    Creates draft in Outlook with proper subject and recipient

    Draft saved in Outlook with "Re:" subject line and correct recipient address.

  7. 7
    Moves original message to "1. Greg" folder

    Organizes processed emails for tracking and prevents duplicate draft creation.
