SEO Link Building Campaign Planner

Make.com Google Sheets OpenAI

Problem

  • Manual selection of outreach domains takes hours per campaign
  • Difficult to balance topical relevance, local relevance, and budget
  • Hard to ensure campaigns meet minimum link requirements
  • No systematic way to prioritize domains across large portfolios

Solution

Built an intelligent campaign planner that fetches customer requirements and portfolio data from Google Sheets, uses OpenAI to score domains based on topical relevance, locality, DR, and traffic, then generates an optimized shortlist that meets budget constraints and minimum link requirements.

Benefits

  • Campaign planning time reduced from 4-6 hours to ~15 minutes
  • Data-driven domain selection with AI scoring
  • Automatic budget optimization across 12 months
  • Guaranteed to meet minimum link requirements
  • Balanced approach (own domains, partner domains, rest of portfolio)

How It Works

  1. 1
    Watches Google Sheets "Config" row for new campaign inputs

    Monitors spreadsheet for customer domain, budget, keywords, and location requirements.

  2. 2
    Fetches all available domains from "Portfolio" sheet

    Retrieves complete inventory of available domains with pricing and metrics.

  3. 3
    Aggregates portfolio data into JSON format

    Structures domain data with DR, traffic, category, region, and pricing information.

  4. 4
    OpenAI analyzes customer context

    AI processes customer location, business type, and monthly budget parameters.

  5. 5
    AI scores each domain for relevance

    Calculates local relevance (+2 for within 50km), thematic fit (+1 for relevant), and general quality scores.

  6. 6
    System selects optimal domain mix within budget

    Prioritizes "own" tier domains, fills with partner/rest, ensures 12-month budget compliance.

  7. 7
    Returns prioritized shortlist with rationale

    Outputs final domain selection with scores, pricing, and selection reasoning for each.
